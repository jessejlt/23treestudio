The Artist

Hello,
I'm Meredith.

I created 23 Tree Studio because I truly believe anyone can paint.

My original tiny landscapes are deeply inspired by the stunning local scenery and colors of Colorado. Using paints, 23K gold leaf, hand-dyed wools, and raw canvas, I pour my impressionist style into each piece, creating a soothing vibe that feels like home.

"
Creativity isn't reserved for the talented few. With the right supplies and a little encouragement, I believe anyone can create something beautiful.
