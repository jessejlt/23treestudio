Hello, I'm Meredith.

I founded 23 Tree Studio because I truly believe anyone can paint.

My artwork is deeply inspired by the stunning local scenery and colors of Colorado. Using acrylics, 23K gold leaf, hand-dyed wools, and raw canvas, I pour my impressionist style into each piece, creating a soothing vibe that feels like home.
